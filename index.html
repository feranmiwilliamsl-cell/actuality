Frequently Asked
Questions

Do I need internet to use Actuality? +

No! Actuality works completely offline. Your data is stored locally on your computer, so you can work without internet.

How long does setup take? +

Most businesses are up and running in under 30 minutes. Just install, create your account, and start adding products.

Is my data secure? +

Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption (the same used by banks) to protect your data. Your information never leaves your computer.

Can multiple people use it? +

Yes! Add team members with different roles (Admin, Manager, Cashier, Staff). Each person gets their own login.

What happens after the free trial? +

After 3 months, you can upgrade to a premium plan to continue using the software. Your data is preserved.

How do I get support? +

Our support team is available via email at support@actuality.cloud. Premium users get priority support.
