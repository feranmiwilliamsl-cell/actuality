Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Actuality ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our inventory management software.

Information We Collect

Actuality is designed to work offline, and we collect minimal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email, and business name provided during registration
  • License Information: Device identifiers for license activation
  • Contact Information: Information you provide when contacting support

Local Data Storage

All your business data (products, inventory, sales, customers) is stored locally on your computer. We do not have access to this data, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

How We Use Your Information

  • To activate and validate your software license
  • To provide customer support
  • To send important product updates and announcements

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
  • Device-bound licensing to prevent unauthorized access
  • Secure local database storage

Third-Party Services

Actuality works 100% offline. We do not share your data with third parties except when required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account data
  • Export your local data at any time

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
